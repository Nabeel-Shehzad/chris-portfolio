UK Research Undergraduate Opportunities (UK students only)

Academic internships

These internships are usually offered by universities and involve working directly with faculty members on ongoing research projects. You may have the chance to assist in lab work and field research.



UK Industry Undergraduate Internships (UK students only)

Industry Internships

Applying for Industry internships allows you to gain hands on experience, contribute in industrial research and a way to start building your network.

European Undergraduate Opportunities

Europe is home to some of the world’s leading research institutions and offers plenty of opportunities for STEM students. This section will help you find internships across the continent.



European Research Undergraduate Internships --SOON--

The European Union funds numerous research programmes that offer research internship opportunities for undergraduate students. These could include students who are currently pursuing a BSc only in Europe, or worldwide including the US and UK.



European Industry Undergraduate Internships --SOON--

The European Union also provides industry undergraduate internship opportunities for undergraduate students. This will help with gaining practical experience in research and developments. There are internships available in multinational corporations, including smaller startups.



Things to consider before applying!

Applying for internships can be competitive, but with the right approach you can stand out. Prepare a strong CV and cover letter that highlight your academic achievements, skills and passion for the subject matter.



NOTE:
An other thing to have in mind, applying for internships in Europe as a non EU national might require some additional steps, especially when it comes to visa regulations. Start by identifying the country where you want to work and then the opportunity you’re going after.
